citiesof stars

I want to tell stories about those who live fast, die young; stars that burn bright and burn fast; the supernova that collapses into itself; truth bleeding under dirt-stained fingernails; cold winters and warm flames; broken crowns of kings and queens; the embers of fallen kingdoms; ashes and smoke and rust; the bloody fingerprints of revolution; finding bones a place to rest; poisoned veins and toxic lungs; violent gods and beautiful monsters of myth; and the desperate beating of bright, young hearts.
